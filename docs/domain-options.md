# Domain Options

`supperware.com` is registered but points to nothing — check ownership via WHOIS.

## Recommended

- **`supperware.kitchen`** — most memorable, completely on-brand, unexpected TLD
- **`supperware.app`** — clear, professional, no explanation needed
- **`supperware.io`** — safe choice, familiar to the likely audience

## Other Options

- `supperware.co` — popular startup suffix, looks clean
- `supperware.cook` — short and punchy
- `supperware.recipes` — food-specific, but try-hard
- `supperware.menu` — works, but same caveat
- `supperware.dev` — good if leaning technical/developer audience
- `supperware.tools` — positions it as a utility

## Notes

Avoid `.recipes` and `.menu` for primary domain — people sometimes distrust
unusual TLDs when entering account credentials. Fine as redirects.

If `supperware.com` isn't ours, parked domains in this category often sell
for a few hundred dollars. Worth pursuing if the product takes off.
